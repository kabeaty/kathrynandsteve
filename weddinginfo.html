Saengerrunde Hall

Saengerrunde Hall was established in 1879 by Saengerrunde, a German singing society, and is a National Historic Site. It's also the oldest continually operating bowling alley in Austin, so be ready to bring your bowling skills at one of its six lanes!

Things to Do in Austin

If you find yourself with some extra time in Austin, there are a lot of cool things to check out. Below is a list we've compiled of some of our favorites that we recommend. Let us know if there's anything else you'd like to try while you're here, and we're happy to make suggestions!

Food

Barbecue

  • Stiles Switch BBQ. This is a laid-black place in North Austin with some delicious BBQ.

Tacos

  • Tacodeli. We get tacos from here most weekends. Our favorites are the Otto and the Jess Special.

  • Torchy's Tacos. We recommend the Fried Avocado if you go here.

Vegetarian

  • Bouldin Creek Cafe. Delicious vegetarian and vegan food in a local, socially conscious, artsy Austin kinda place.
